About me

Internship at CFE Nuclear Plant Laguna Verde

In 2016 i applied and got accepted as an intern in Laguna Verde in the Software Development Department, while in there i got the task of learning and applying the Angular framework as the front-end, Java as back-end and use their INFORMIX-IBM database to develop a new app that would use the 360-degree feedback to evaluate the high-directives employees in the organization. At the time of this the Department was developing with Adobe Flex, after they saw the potential that Angular had and knowing that Flash browser support was coming to an end they got encouraged to make the switch to Angular, They now only develop new apps with Angular and started outsourcing jobs to migrate the old apps to Angular because soon most modern browsers will stop working with flash.
